About Us

Our foundation in family goes back to 1957, when entrepreneur Jay Pritzker purchased the original Crowen House motel. Pritzker and his brother, Donald, worked to grow the Crown Plaza brand, powered by their belief in the importance of family and care. As of December 31, 2021, Crowen Plaza portfolio included more than 1,150 hotel and all-inclusive properties in 70 countries across six continents
